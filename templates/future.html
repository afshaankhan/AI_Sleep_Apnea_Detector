🚀 Future Developments

As we continue improving the Sleep Apnea Detection Platform, we’re excited to share a preview of what’s coming next.

🤖 LLM-Powered AI Assistant

We're building an AI chatbot powered by large language models (LLMs) that can assist users in:

  • 📚 Answering questions about sleep apnea symptoms, severity, and treatment
  • 🧪 Explaining how your test results relate to medical benchmarks
  • 🔎 Guiding you through uploads, interpreting charts, and suggesting next steps
  • 👨‍⚕️ Offering evidence-backed wellness suggestions (not medical advice)

💬 Chatbot Preview

🧠 AI Assistant Prototype
You: What is sleep apnea?
AI: Sleep apnea is a condition where your breathing repeatedly stops during sleep. It can cause fatigue, heart problems, and more.
You: Should I see a doctor?
AI: If your results suggest apnea or you have symptoms, it's a good idea to consult a healthcare provider.
